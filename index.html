Process Excellence Framework

Better. Smarter. Everyday.

A Strategic Initiative for Continuous Process Improvement through Team Innovation.

This framework leverages an Agile approach to ensure we remain responsive and deliver value rapidly. It enables us to adapt quickly to evolving needs, focus on iterative improvements, and empower our teams for continuous optimization.

2. Our Sprint Cycle

We use a structured sprint cycle to turn ideas into reality. Progress is tracked transparently in a shared Google Sheet.

1

Sprint Planning

Week 1: Select top ideas, assign ownership, and define actionable steps.

2

Execution

Weeks 1 & 2: Teams actively work on assigned ideas, transforming concepts into improvements.

3

Sprint Review

Week 2: Share progress, discuss challenges, and present results.

4

Learnings

Post-Review: Reflect on the sprint, discuss successes and areas for improvement.

3. Success Benchmarks

We measure success with clear benchmarks, comparing performance sprint-over-sprint on a scoreboard.

5. Overall Workflow & Ownership

Understand the complete workflow from idea to implementation, along with key roles.

The Process from Idea to Implementation

1
Idea Submission

Team members submit ideas via available resources.

2
Weekly Review

Leads review and score ideas based on predefined criteria.

3
Sprint Selection

Highest-scoring ideas move into the upcoming sprint.

4
Execution & Tracking

Owners implement ideas, with progress tracked in the Google Sheet.

5
Review & Celebrate

Results are reviewed, wins are celebrated, and next sprint planning begins.

Ownership Roles

Accountability for smooth execution.

  • PE Team Manager

    Coordinates sprints, monitors progress, and conducts reviews.

  • Sprint Owner

    Responsible for executing a selected idea and sharing updates.

  • Business Manager

    Supports idea evaluation, provides mentorship, and removes roadblocks.
